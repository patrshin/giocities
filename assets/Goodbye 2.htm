                                                                     Chapter Two

Three Weeks Later




"What do you mean you're going to Sweden?" Jessica shouted in anger.

"We're going to lay down some tracks for the new record." Kevin said calmly.

"But what about the arrangements for our wedding Kevin! We're supposed to get married in less then a month. What's going to happen? On the morning of the wedding, am I going to wake up to find a note that says 'Sorry honey I had to go to Sweden.'? I don't think so Kevin! That will not happen to me." Jessica raged on.

"I'm part of a group, Jessica. I can't not go." Kevin said softly his sharp features clouded with guilt. Jessica almost felt bad when she saw the remorse on his face.

"Kevin, I've tried to understand but you're not only part of a group you're about to become part of a marriage and I'm beginning to think you're not ready for that." Jessica said sadly.

"What are you trying to say, Jess? You don't want to be with me anymore?" Kevin questioned.

Jessica went over to where he stood bags packed and coat on ready, to run out like he had over and over again in the last few weeks.

"It's not that I don't want to be with you Kev, it's that I can't be with you and your group. I can't keep saying goodbye and then putting my life on hold until you get back." She said staring up into his solemn face.

"Music is a major part of my life Jess. I can't just throw it all away. I don't want to lose you but I'm not going to choose between you and my dream. I hope you're still here when I get back." He said and then picked up his bags and went out the door.

Jessica watched the door close behind him and then leaned her head against the cool wood finally letting the tears of pent up frustration stream down her face.
